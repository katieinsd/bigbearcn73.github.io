Frequently Asked Questions:

  • Do you repair any other kind of phones, that are not listed on the website?

    We repair most phones and tablets. Feel free to give us a call and we will be happy to give you an estimate.

  • Are any of repairs under warranty?

    All of our repairs come with a lifetime warranty. Conditions apply. Any water or physical damage is not covered.

  • Do you repair iPods and other Apple products?

    We certainly do, please give us a call for an estimate.

  • I have more than one issue on my phone, do you offer bundle deals?

    Yes, we most certainly do have discounts on multiple issues affecting your device. Give us a call to find out more.

  • I dropped my phone and it has become unresponsive to touch, does the LCD and Glass repair fix this issue?

    Yes, in most cases all your phone needs is a new LCD and glass replacement.

  • Can my phone be repaired while I wait?

    Most iPhone and Samsung repairs take around 30-45min, you are welcome to wait. iPad repairs are usually done the same or the next day.

  • Do I need to make an appointment to bring my device in for a repair?

    No appointments are necessary for iPhones and iPads, other devices depend on part availability.
